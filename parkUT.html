ParkUT Mobile App

Jan 2022 - Present

Automatic parking space detection solution is one of the smartest and most modern solutions available today. The system uses many modern technological devices to bring satisfaction to both vehicle owners and managers. The solution is applied to many parking lots and the equipment system is also increasingly improved to suit the needs. Information about parking spaces is updated every second, so you can know if the parking lot is available or not to make a decision to enter the parking lot or find the nearest other parking space.

According to WTVG in July 2021, following the tragedy of the Surfside Condominium collapse in Florida and recent inspections for yearly maintenance, the University of Toledo expedited its preparations to remove its parking structures. An estimated 6,700 parking spaces are available following the demolition. USNews states that 80% of the 19,000 University of Toledo students lived off campus during the Fall 2020 semester. With decreased supply and ever-present demand for parking, smarter management and increased data collection in parking lots is key for universities and other organizations to better utilize and understand their parking offerings.

The principle idea of our product is to use computer vision and AI to relay to an end user how full a parking lot is. Our product will then enable a user to plan where they will park prior to starting their commute. Our capacity data will also be available to school administrators, playing an important role in the planning of future additions to school infrastructure.

A camera system which uses AI to detect parking lot capacity and open spaces. Real-time data is presented through an app to allow ease of finding parking. Furthermore, it will provide the shortest path to the closest available parking spot. The app will also gather data over time to predict when a lot is likely to be full and will inform the user of any lot closures. Our statistics will also be available to the school, allowing detailed parking lot information to be used in strategic planning for future construction.
