Emotion Recognition

Sep 2020 - Oct 2020

One of the various face recognition technologies that have evolved and expanded throughout time is emotion recognition. Currently, a certain program can analyze and evaluate a person's facial emotions thanks to the usage of facial emotion recognition software. This program mimics the way a human brain works by using sophisticated picture dispensation, which enables it to recognize emotions as well.

Artificial intelligence, or AI, is what recognizes and studies various facial expressions so that it may use them in conjunction with information that is provided to it. This helps authorities identify a person's emotions using just technology, which is essential for a number of tasks including investigations and interviews.

We use several analytics technique to analyze whether a person is happy or not based on their photos.
